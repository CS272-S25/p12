The GOAT Debate: Jordan vs. LeBron

The debate over the greatest basketball player of all time (GOAT) continues to rage on. Is it Michael Jordan, with his perfect record in the Finals and unmatched competitive fire? Or is it LeBron James, with his incredible all-around game, longevity, and statistical dominance?
